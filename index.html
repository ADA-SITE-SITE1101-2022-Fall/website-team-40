Team Rocket's Hour of code

What is Hour of Code

Hour of Code is an annual event about coding. Its main purpose is to spread interest in computer science in diverse environments, especially among young girls and racial minorities. It uses not only online activities but also paper-based games making it available for computer free environments. Hour of Code is made for all ages starting from pre-readers to highschoolers and older. It has been conducted in more than 180 countries and 45 times in Azerbaijan in 2022. We, Team Rocket Hosted one of these events. With this site we want to commemorate our experience and inspire future students to participate in this educational movement.

About us

As we mentioned above, we conducted this event at School #274. Although it is located quite far from the capital and the weather was poor, we did not hesitate to execute our mission. In addition, students' enthusiasm encouraged us to do even better than what we had planned. We would like to convey our gratitude to teacher Vusala Aliyeva and principal Nariman Mursaliyev for letting us carry out this event.
