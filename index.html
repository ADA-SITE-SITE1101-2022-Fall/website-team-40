What is Hour of Code

Hour of Code is an annual event about coding. Its main purpose is to spread interest in computer science in diverse environments, especially among young girls and racial minorities.
It uses not only online activities but also paper-based games making it available for computer free environments.
Hour of Code is made for all ages starting from pre-readers to highschoolers and older.
It has been conducted in more than 180 countries and 45 times in Azerbaijan in 2022.
We, Team Rocket Hosted one of these events. With this site we want to commemorate our experience and inspire future students to participate in this educational movement.
